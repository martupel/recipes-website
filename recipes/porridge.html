Mushu's Porridge

Description

Mulan's breakfast, made by Mushu on the morning of her first day of training in the army.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 4 cups water
  • few slices of ginger & scallion
  • 1/2 lb fish
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tbsp water
  • 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • topping: eggs, bacon, scallion, mushroom, soy sauce

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil with your ginger & scallion. Stir in the washed rice, once it's boiling let it simmer for 20 mins until the rice is fully cooked.
  2. In the meantime, marinate the fish with cornstarch, oil, water, & soy sauce. Also prepare your toppings!
  3. At this point the rice should be fully cooked, whisk the pot vigorously to break up the rice until it reaches your desired texture.
  4. Add the fish slices one at a time, stirring gently until they're just cooked through.
  5. Serve the porridge along with your toppings!
